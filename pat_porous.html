Porous Extrusion

Chulalongkorn, 2013

Taking the existing ephemeral qualities of a densely populated area in Hong Kong in consideration, the Porous Extrusion aimed to create dwellings that allow the right amount of ventilation, sunlight, and the view of sky for every residential units on the tower without ruining these qualities of the existing site.
